The AI HR Automation Platform was a project that aimed to simplify the process of recruiting for HR personnel by using automation technology and AI to replace otherwise menial and time consuming tasks.

In this project, I took the role of lead designer, assigning roles and collaborating with team members to design the flow of the entire system. Weekly meetings were held to ensure all members of the team were headed in the same direction. The occasional training was also held where I went over design concepts and Figma fundamentals to members who weren't familiar with them.

I created mockups of each of the pages to better communicate my ideas to both team members and managers. To demonstrate the validity of my project, I also created various proof of concept prototypes, including an AI Interviewer, AI Job Description Generator, PDF extracctor, and AI Skill Tagger.
