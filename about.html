About Me

Hi! My name is Alex Han. I graduated from the University of Sydney with a Bachelor's of Design Computing. I was born in the United States, but grew up in Taiwan in a bilingual environment, making me fluent in both English and Mandarin Chinese. Through self study and interaction with native speakers, I have also developed my Japanese to a conversational level.

Over the years, I've had the opportunity to participate in several internships across the world, honing my design skills in a professional context. Companies I've interned at include Deloitte and Zero One Tech in Taipei, Jetabroad in Sydney, and Little Adam Inc from Shanghai (remotely).

I have a strong passion for learning and I try to broaden my experiences whenever I can. Over the years, I've volunteered for various groups, including the Rotary Club, Keelung for a Walk, Oki Olive, and the Founder Institute.

Professionally, I hope to use my experiences to develop better user experiences and to work towards a more sustainable future for the world.
