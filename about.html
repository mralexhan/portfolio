About Me

Hi, I'm Alex Han, an aspiring UI/UX designer. I was born in the United States, but grew up in Taiwan, studying in a bilingual school from elementary to high school. This has provided me the unique opportunity to be a part of two different cultures growing up. As a result, I have the ability to speak two languages fluently (English and Mandarin Chinese), and have also developed a curiosity towards learning about different kinds of people.

It is this curiosity, coupled with my natural desire to help others, is what led to my interest in UI/UX Design. The often personal nature of design is a great way to learn about the needs, desires, and stories of different people. I also love thinking up solutions to solve problems or streamline processes. I hope that, through my designs, I can find a way to make life easier for others.
